Quant

Quant is a textbook-style resource for learning financial algorithms, investing methods, trading frameworks, and quantitative models from first principles to advanced implementation.

How This Textbook Works

Each chapter is designed as a guided tutorial page with a practical sequence: concept, intuition, implementation ideas, and an empirical testing section. Over time, this creates a complete learning track rather than isolated blog posts.

The left sidebar combines global navigation, chapter progression, and section anchors so readers can move through the material quickly without losing context.
